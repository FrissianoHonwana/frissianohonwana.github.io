Frissiano Honwana

Biostatistician

PhD in Public Health (Biostatistics)

My research focuses on prediction modelling using biomarkers to improve healthcare outcomes through advanced statistical techniques. I work at the intersection of biomarker science and precision medicine where I develop and refine models that improve disease prediction and patient care, advancing diagnostic accuracy and optimising treatment strategies.

Consultation

Consulting is offered to support researchers at every stage, from initial design through to final reporting.

Early engagement is strongly encouraged. Ideally, clients should make contact before the project begins. An initial scoping consultation is offered at no charge to assess your needs and tailor the support to your specific project.
Research Design

Guidance on study design, sample size calculation, and data collection strategies to ensure your research is methodologically sound from the outset.

Data Management

Practical support for data capturing, cleaning, and management to maintain data integrity throughout your project.

Statistical Analysis

A full range of statistical analyses and support with data interpretation:

  • Descriptive and quantitative analysis
  • Hypothesis testing and statistical inference
  • Regression analysis
  • Longitudinal and survival analysis
  • Risk score development and predictive modelling
  • Data interpretation and analytical report writing

Who We Work With

Honours students Research Masters PhD candidates Postdoctoral researchers Academic & research staff
